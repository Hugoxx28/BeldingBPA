About Us

Belding Green Initiative is a group of juniors and seniors from Belding High School, located in Belding, Michigan. Interested in website design, the vast fields and opportunities it offers, and creating masterpieces through every line of code, we worked together wo produce this website

The team is presenting this website to the 2022 BPA competition with the topic of: tips, tricks, resources for the community to reduce its carbon footprint, and how to make Belding a healthier town to live in.
